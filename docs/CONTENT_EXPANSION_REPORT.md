# Content Expansion Report — Mystery Agency

**Date:** 2026-07-15
Evidence: `src/server/services/story-init.ts` (`grep -c "id: 'chapter'" = 10`).

## Summary

The campaign grew from **5 → 10 chapters**, a complete two-book arc titled **The Red Fox Files**. Each chapter has a strong setup, **4 interconnected clues** (mixed EVIDENCE / TESTIMONY / DOCUMENT types), supports multiple plausible theories, and feeds a canon outcome that carries motifs forward.

## The arc

**Book One — The Red Fox (Ch. 1–5)**
1. **The Midnight Heist** — museum diamond; impossible insider job; first paper-fox calling card. *(suspect)*
2. **The Vanishing Canvas** — gallery theft in plain sight; "R. Vulpes" signs the book. *(suspect)*
3. **The Harbor Job** — a 30-ton container disappears; the foreman's keycard used after hours. *(method)*
4. **The Locked Study** — the collector attacked, ledger stolen, whisper of "twins". *(motive)*
5. **The First Fox** — name the first Fox — but the burner phone said there were **two**. *(prediction, midpoint twist)*

**Book Two — The Second Fox (Ch. 6–10)**
6. **The Copycat** — a mirror-image (right-handed) fox strikes days after the arrest. *(suspect)*
7. **The Insurance Trail** — over-insured forgeries, shell companies, silent partner "V." *(motive)*
8. **The Safehouse** — a forgery base, a reopened tunnel, "two people who look alike". *(method)*
9. **The Double Bluff** — a too-perfect frame job built left- AND right-handed. *(suspect)*
10. **The Last Fox** — DNA proves fraternal twins; "V." is the mastermind; predict the finale. *(prediction, true finale)*

## Interconnection (threads that pay off)

- **Paper foxes:** left-handed folds (Book One) → right-handed mirror (Ch. 6) → both hands in the frame job (Ch. 9) → fraternal-twin DNA (Ch. 10).
- **The initial "V.":** gallery guest "R. Vulpes" (Ch. 2) → silent partner "V." (Ch. 7) → same handwriting as the collector's letters (Ch. 10).
- **Insider access / alibis:** impossible-but-legitimate credentials (Ch. 1, 5) → the fraud motive (Ch. 7) → the reveal (Ch. 10).
- **The tunnel & harbor:** harbor container (Ch. 3) → reopened tunnel + lockers (Ch. 8) → the "stolen" originals were hidden there all along (Ch. 10).

## Design intent

- Every chapter's clues deliberately support **≥2 credible theories** so the community vote is meaningful (no single forced answer; canon is community-decided).
- Two clear finales (Ch. 5 midpoint, Ch. 10 grand finale) give the campaign shape and a satisfying end.
- `theory_type` varies (suspect / method / motive / prediction) to keep submissions fresh.

## Progression & rewards (unchanged, now spanning 10 chapters)

Submitting (+10 XP), voting (+2), votes received (+5), canonization (+100), streaks, 6 ranks, 5 badges — now across a 10-chapter run. Auto-progression (12h/phase) plus moderator control drives the campaign start-to-finish.
