# Privacy Policy — Mystery Agency

**Last updated:** 2026-07-15

Mystery Agency ("the game", "we") is a game that runs on Reddit via the Reddit Developer Platform (Devvit). This policy explains what data the game processes and why. By playing, you agree to this policy.

## 1. Who this applies to

Anyone who opens or plays Mystery Agency inside a Reddit post on a subreddit where a moderator has installed the app.

## 2. What data we process

The game processes only the data needed to run gameplay, all tied to your Reddit account through Devvit:

- **Reddit user ID and username** — to identify you, save your progress, and show you on leaderboards.
- **Gameplay data you create** — theories you submit, votes you cast, XP, rank, badges, submission streak, and daily activity dates.
- **Local device preferences** — a small amount of data stored in your browser's `localStorage` (e.g., a notification "last seen phase" marker and an optional local admin-UI flag). This never leaves your device.

We do **not** collect: email addresses, passwords, real names, phone numbers, payment information, precise location, or any data from outside the game. The game does **not** use third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

## 3. How we use the data

Solely to operate the game: run the theory/voting/canon loop, award XP and badges, rank players, render leaderboards and profiles, enforce fair-play limits (one theory per chapter, daily vote caps), and let subreddit moderators administer the game.

## 4. Where data is stored

Game data is stored in **Reddit's Devvit-managed Redis**, hosted and secured by Reddit as part of the platform. We do not operate a separate database or export data to external services.

## 5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Aggregated, in-game information (such as your username and stats appearing on public leaderboards, or a theory you submit) is visible to other players by design, as part of the collaborative game.

## 6. Data retention & deletion

Game data persists while the app is installed on a subreddit. A subreddit **moderator can reset the game**, which clears theories, canon selections, and phase state. Because identity and hosting are provided by Reddit, account-level data handling also follows **Reddit's Privacy Policy** and your Reddit account settings.

## 7. Children's privacy

The game is intended for Reddit users who meet Reddit's minimum age requirements. It is not directed at children under 13.

## 8. Your choices

- You can stop playing at any time.
- You can clear local device preferences in-game via **Settings → Reset Local Data**.
- For account-level data, use your Reddit account controls and Reddit's data tools.

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly. Material changes will be noted in the game's release notes.

## 10. Contact

Questions about this policy should be directed to the moderators of the subreddit hosting the game, or the app maintainer via the project repository.
